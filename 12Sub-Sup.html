Subscript and Superscript in HTML

Subscript is used to define a text that appears lower than the baseline. For example, the chemical formula for water is represented as:

H2O

Superscript is used for text that appears higher than the baseline. For instance, in mathematics, exponents are represented as:

x2 + y2 = z2

You can also use both subscript and superscript together, for example:

E=mc2
