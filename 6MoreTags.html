Interesting Animal Facts

Here are some interesting facts about various animals:

Lions are often called the "king of the jungle."

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth.

Giraffes have the longest necks of any animal, allowing them to reach high trees.

Combining formats:

The cheetah is the fastest land animal, capable of reaching speeds up to 70 mph.
Pandas primarily eat bamboo and spend around 14 hours a day eating.
Whales are the largest animals in the world, with the blue whale being the biggest.
Owls have the ability to rotate their heads up to 270 degrees.

Here are some fun facts listed with line breaks:
Penguins can't fly but are excellent swimmers.
Dolphins are known for their high intelligence.
Kangaroos can leap over 30 feet in a single bound.
Tigers have unique striped patterns on their fur, much like human fingerprints.
Wolves communicate through howls, which can be heard over long distances.
Butterflies taste with their feet.
